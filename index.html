The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a inclusive nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Our vision is one of inclusivity and empowerment. Every individual should thrive through access to education, healthcare, and meaningful work. Technology and innovation will bridge gaps, fostering a connected, compassionate world. Businesses must operate with a social conscience, prioritizing people and the planet alongside profit. Social businesses will drive sustainable development and positive change. Guided by equity, justice, and compassion, we can collaboratively address global challenges like climate change and economic instability. Together, we can build a world where everyone lives a life of dignity and fulfillment.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
